Abhishek Badki

I'm a Senior Research Scientist in the Learning and Perception Team at NVIDIA Research. I completed my Ph.D. in the ECE department at UC Santa Barbara, where my adviser was Pradeep Sen. My Ph.D. research has benefited in many ways from the continued mentorship and support of Orazio Gallo and Jan Kautz. I was fortunate to be awarded the NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship.

Research

My research lies at the intersection of 3D vision, graphics and machine learning. My current focus is on 4D dynamic scene understanding, particularly for autonomous navigation and robotics applications.
